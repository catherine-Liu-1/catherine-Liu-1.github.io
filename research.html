Research

Statistical learning, Bayesian inference, high-dimensional data, medical imaging, and applications in health and environmental studies.

Methodologies and Theories

  • Imaging data and statistical learning.
  • Bayesian inference and factor-analysis learning.
  • Matrix and tensor factor models for high-dimensional data.
  • Functional, longitudinal, and multivariate data.
  • Earlier work on missing data, survival data, and biomarker studies.

Applications

  • Medical image data and interpretable learning.
  • Biomedical empirical studies in ophthalmology and type-2 diabetes.
  • Multi-omics and health sciences.
  • Environmental statistics in hydrology and climate.
